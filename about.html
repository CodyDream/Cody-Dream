About Cody Dream Studios
We Build Tools & Games

Cody Dream Studios is an independent development studio focused on creating high-quality Unity assets and original games. Our mission is to help fellow creators move faster and deliver polished player experiences through robust tools and clear documentation.

Originality & Ownership

We take creative integrity seriously. All assets, games, and tutorials featured on this site are original works developed by Cody Dream Studios. In instances where third-party resources are utilized, we ensure full compliance with licenses and provide proper attribution.

Our professional assets are distributed under the Standard Unity Asset Store EULA, ensuring a reliable and industry-standard licensing experience for all users.
